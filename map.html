Public Transport Info - The Essentials

TransJakarta
  • Corridor 1: Blok M - Kota, stop at Monas/Balaikota.
  • Corridor 2: Pulogadung - Harmoni, stop at Harmoni.
KRL Commuter Line
  • Stop at Gondangdia or Juanda for Monas.
  • Stop at Jakarta Kota Station for Kota Tua.
MRT Jakarta
  • Stop at Bundaran HI, continue with TransJakarta or taxi.

Jakarta Vibes

Jakarta is the ultimate mix of history, culture, and modern life. From iconic landmarks to foodie adventures, this city’s got it all. Dive in and explore!

  • Monas: The National Monument, symbolizing Indonesia's independence, with a museum and observation deck.
  • Kota Tua: The Old Town, rich in colonial history, museums, street art, and culinary delights.
