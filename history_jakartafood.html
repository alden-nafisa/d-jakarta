Jakarta's Culinary Journey

Jakarta's food culture is a melting pot of flavors, influenced by its diverse history and communities. From Betawi classics like Soto Betawi and Kerak Telor to international cuisines, the city's culinary scene reflects its vibrant heritage.

Traditional markets and street vendors offer authentic tastes, while modern restaurants showcase innovative dishes. Jakarta's food journey is a testament to its adaptability and creativity, blending old and new to create a unique gastronomic experience.

Explore Jakarta's Food

Discover the rich culinary heritage of Jakarta through its famous dishes and food destinations.

  • Soto Betawi: A creamy beef soup with coconut milk, a Betawi specialty.
  • Kerak Telor: A traditional omelette made with glutinous rice and egg.
  • Street Food: From satay to fried rice, Jakarta's street food scene is vibrant and diverse.
