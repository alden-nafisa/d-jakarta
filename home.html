Jakarta’s Iconic Spots - The Lowdown

Jakarta is buzzing with iconic spots that scream history and culture. Two must-visits are Monas and Kota Tua. Here’s the scoop:

  • Monas: The National Monument, a legit symbol of Indonesia’s independence, with a museum and killer views from the observation deck.
  • Kota Tua: The Old Town, packed with colonial vibes, museums, street art, and foodie heaven.
